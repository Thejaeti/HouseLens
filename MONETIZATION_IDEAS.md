# HouseLens Monetization Ideas

## In-App Revenue

### Freemium Model
- Free tier: ~5 lookups per day
- Paid tier: unlimited lookups ($2.99/month or $19.99/year)

### Premium Features
- Free users get the address only
- Paid users get price estimates, tax info, sale history, etc.
- Would require a real estate data API (Zillow API, ATTOM, RealtyMole)

## Real Estate Partnerships

### Agent Referrals
- "Connect with an agent" button after a lookup
- Partner with local agents or brokerages for referral fees
- Referral fees can be $500+ per closed deal

### Mortgage/Lender Leads
- "Interested in this home? Get pre-approved" prompt
- Mortgage companies pay well for qualified leads

## Advertising

### Display Ads (AdMob)
- Show ads on the address card after lookup
- Real estate is one of the highest-paying ad categories

### Sponsored Listings
- Local agents pay to appear when users look up houses in their area

## Data (Longer Term)
- Aggregated, anonymized data on which neighborhoods attract interest
- Valuable to real estate companies, developers, and investors

## Recommended Starting Point
Freemium model (limited free lookups + subscription) combined with an agent referral button. Low implementation effort with high revenue potential.
